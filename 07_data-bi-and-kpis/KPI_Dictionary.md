# KPI Dictionary (Healthcare Supply Chain Enablement)

## Purpose
Provide clear, consistent definitions for metrics used to evaluate training effectiveness, system adoption, and operational performance.

---

## Training Completion Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of assigned staff completing required training.
**Why it matters:** Establishes baseline readiness.
**Data Source:** LMS or training tracker.

## Time-to-Proficiency
**Definition:** Average time for new hires to meet defined competency standards.
**Why it matters:** Directly impacts productivity and error rates.
**Data Source:** Manager readiness checklists.

## Requisition Error Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of requisitions requiring correction or rework.
**Why it matters:** Indicates training and process clarity.
**Data Source:** eProcurement system reports.

## Receiving Cycle Time
**Definition:** Time from physical receipt to inventory availability.
**Why it matters:** Affects fulfillment and clinical support.
**Data Source:** Receiving and inventory systems.
