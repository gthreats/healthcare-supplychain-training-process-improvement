# Role-Based Learning Paths

## Purpose
Ensure onboarding is tailored to functional responsibilities while maintaining shared understanding across Supply Chain.

## Example Roles
- Buyer / Procurement Specialist
- Contracting & Compliance
- Receiving / Distribution
- Inventory Management
- Supply Chain Analyst
- eProcurement Support

## Core Learning (All Roles)
- Supply Chain overview and patient-care impact
- Compliance and security fundamentals
- eProcurement workflow awareness
- SOP navigation and exception handling

## Role-Specific Focus (Example – Buyer)
- Requisition review and PO creation
- Contract usage and compliance
- Vendor communication standards
- Financial controls and approval workflows

## How this is applied
Managers assign learning paths based on role while reinforcing cross-functional awareness to reduce handoff errors.

## Interview Talking Point
“This allows me to scale onboarding without treating every role the same, while still reinforcing shared accountability.”
