# 30-60-90 Day New Hire Enablement (Healthcare Supply Chain)

## Purpose
Provide a structured onboarding framework that builds foundational knowledge, system competence, and independent performance while maintaining compliance and patient-safety awareness.

---

## First 30 Days – Orientation & Foundation
**Focus:** Understanding the environment and workflows

Key Objectives:
- Understand Supply Chain’s role in patient care and institutional operations
- Learn core policies, compliance requirements, and security expectations
- Gain introductory exposure to eProcurement and related systems
- Observe end-to-end workflows (requisition to receipt)

Success Indicators:
- Completion of required training modules
- Demonstrated understanding of basic workflows
- Ability to navigate systems with supervision

---

## Days 31–60 – Skill Development & Supervised Execution
**Focus:** Performing core tasks with guidance

Key Objectives:
- Execute role-specific tasks with supervision
- Understand upstream/downstream handoffs
- Apply SOPs accurately in daily work
- Identify common errors and escalation paths

Success Indicators:
- Reduced error rate on assigned tasks
- Fewer support requests for basic functions
- Manager validation of competency milestones

---

## Days 61–90 – Independent Performance & Optimization
**Focus:** Consistent execution and problem-solving

Key Objectives:
- Perform core responsibilities independently
- Resolve routine issues using SOPs and job aids
- Contribute improvement ideas and feedback
- Demonstrate accountability for quality and service standards

Success Indicators:
- Independent task execution
- Stable productivity and accuracy metrics
- Readiness sign-off by manager

---

## Interview Talking Point
“This framework lets leaders see exactly where new hires are succeeding or struggling and intervene early—before errors impact operations or compliance.”
