# Standard Deliverable Templates

> **Purpose:** Ensure all outputs are consistent, scalable, and easy to maintain.

---

## Core Deliverable Types
- Standard Work (1–2 pages)
- Quick Reference Guides (front/back)
- Microlearning modules (5–8 minutes)
- Process maps and visual aids

---

## Template Standards
- Clear owner and version control
- Plain language, role-specific
- Designed for reuse and updates
- Stored in a single source of truth

---

## Outcome
Templates reduce rework, improve adoption, and allow new team members to produce value quickly.
