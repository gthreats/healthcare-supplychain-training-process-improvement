# Customer Service & Quality

This folder defines how Supply Chain education, processes, and systems support high-quality service to faculty, staff, and clinical partners.

The focus is on consistency, responsiveness, and professionalism—ensuring operational excellence aligns with the mission of patient care.

## What’s Inside
- Service standards and response expectations
- Quality management and escalation framework
- Tools supporting reliable internal customer service

## How this is applied
These standards guide daily interactions, issue resolution, and performance expectations across Supply Chain teams.

## My thought process on this as a Manager
Strong customer service is not accidental—it is trained, measured, and reinforced through clear standards and accountability.
