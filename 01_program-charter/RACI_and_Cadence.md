# RACI & Cadence (Supply Chain Enablement)

## RACI (High-Level)

| Activity | Responsible (R) | Accountable (A) | Consulted (C) | Informed (I) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Training intake & prioritization | Enablement Manager | Supply Chain Director | IR, Ops, Finance | Stakeholders |
| New hire onboarding curriculum | Enablement Manager | Supply Chain Director | Ops Managers | HR/Teams |
| Cross-functional training modules | Enablement Manager | Supply Chain Director | SMEs, IR | End users |
| SOP creation/updates | Enablement Manager + Process Owner | Process Owner | Compliance, IR | Staff |
| System change readiness | Enablement Manager + IR Lead | IR Lead | Ops, SMEs | Users |
| KPI reporting | Enablement Manager | Supply Chain Director | Analytics/Finance | Leaders |

## Cadence

### Monthly Enablement Forum (60 minutes)
Purpose: align priorities, approve training/SOP updates, and review performance trends.
Standing agenda:
1. KPI review (adoption, errors, cycle time, tickets)
2. Upcoming system changes (IR roadmap and readiness needs)
3. Training requests and prioritization
4. SOP/policy updates requiring education
5. Risks, escalations, and decisions

### Change-Go-Live Readiness (as needed)
- T-30: impact assessment and comms plan
- T-14: training delivery + job aids
- T-7: readiness checklist review
- Go-live week: hypercare triage and daily touchpoints

### Quarterly Review (executive summary)
- What improved, what did not, and what we are changing next quarter
