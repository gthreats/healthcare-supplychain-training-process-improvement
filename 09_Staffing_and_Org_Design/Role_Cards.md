# Role Cards

> **Purpose:** Define roles by outcomes, responsibilities, and interfaces rather than job titles alone.

---

## Supply Chain Enablement Manager (Player-Coach)

### Purpose
Establish and lead the enablement function while ensuring execution, governance, and measurable impact.

### Primary Outcomes
- Reduced time-to-proficiency  
- Standardized training and processes  
- Improved operational performance  

### Key KPIs
- Training completion and proficiency metrics  
- Process adoption rates  
- Delivery against roadmap  
- Stakeholder satisfaction  

### Interfaces
Operations leadership, HR, IT, Compliance, Finance

---

## Instructional Design and Content Lead

### Purpose
Translate operational knowledge into scalable, role-based learning assets.

### Primary Outcomes
- Consistent onboarding experience  
- Reduced reliance on SMEs for training  

### Key KPIs
- Content utilization rates  
- Post-training performance improvement  
- Reduction in repeat errors  

---

## Process Improvement and Adoption Specialist

### Purpose
Standardize, improve, and embed operational workflows.

### Primary Outcomes
- Clear, accessible standard work  
- Improved compliance and quality  
- Reduced variation across sites  

---

## Program Coordinator / Analyst

### Purpose
Enable execution throughput and performance visibility.

### Primary Outcomes
- Organized intake and prioritization  
- Reliable reporting  
- Smooth execution cadence  

---

## My thought process on this as a Manager

I design roles around outcomes and interfaces so accountability is clear from day one. This prevents overlap, reduces friction with operations, and allows the team to scale without confusion as demand grows.
