# Train-the-Trainer Playbook

## Purpose
Scale training delivery by enabling supervisors and SMEs to reinforce learning consistently.

## Trainer Selection
- Strong process knowledge
- Credibility with peers
- Clear communication skills

## Trainer Responsibilities
- Deliver standardized content
- Reinforce SOPs and job aids
- Capture questions and feedback
- Escalate gaps or risks

## Support Provided
- Facilitator guides
- Slide outlines or job aids
- Q&A reference materials

## Application
Used during system changes, onboarding expansions, and large-scale rollouts.

## My thought process on this as a Manager
Train-the-trainer allows consistent messaging while keeping education close to the work.
