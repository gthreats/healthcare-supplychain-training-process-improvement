# Go-Live Readiness Checklist (Healthcare Supply Chain)

## Purpose
Confirm operational readiness prior to system go-live.

## Training Readiness
- Required training delivered and tracked
- Job aids distributed
- Managers briefed on changes

## Process Readiness
- SOPs updated and approved
- Exception handling defined
- Escalation paths confirmed

## System Readiness
- User access validated
- Test scenarios completed
- Reporting and controls verified

## Communication Readiness
- Go-live communications sent
- Support contacts published
- Downtime or risk windows communicated

## Go / No-Go Decision
- Risks reviewed
- Leadership sign-off obtained

## My thought process on this as a Manager
This checklist protects operations by ensuring we do not go live based on assumptions or pressure alone.
